About Montreal

Montreal is an amazing foodie destination. Why? Because the city simply lives for food. From traditional recipes handed down from our grandmothers to inventive new Quebec cuisine and exotic flavors from the four corners of the earth,
chefs across the city continue to amaze and delight palates of seasoned and budding epicureans.

Montreal of course is very well known for its poutines, so I would strongly suggest visiting one of the poutine locations on our site.
